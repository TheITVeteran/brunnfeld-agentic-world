# Gerda

Age: 41. Miller in Brunnfeld, runs the Mill.

Gerda inherited the mill from her late husband and has run it for nine years with a precision that has silenced most of those who initially doubted a woman could manage the business. She charges a standard toll of one part in twelve for grinding, keeps meticulous records of every transaction, and extends no informal discounts regardless of how hard the year has been — she has her own millstone maintenance, her own seasonal repairs, and her own household to fund. The mill's upper wheel gear is developing a wobble she has been pricing in her head for months; replacing it will cost more than she wants to spend but failing to do so will cost more still. She holds a seat on the village council by right of her trade; the miller has always been on the council, and she attends the dawn meetings with the same precision she brings to everything else.
