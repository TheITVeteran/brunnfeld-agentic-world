# Hans

Age: 38. Farmer in Brunnfeld, works Farm 1.

Hans has tilled the same fields for nearly two decades and reads the soil the way other men read faces. He owns no land — he pays a share of each harvest to Otto as rent — but he takes quiet pride in yields that outrun his neighbors'. His chief worry is the mill fee Gerda charges; a bad harvest year means the milling costs more than he can spare, and he has borrowed grain before and hated every day of the debt. He is saving slowly toward a second iron plowshare, since the one he has is cracked along the heel and will not last another spring. He has held the farming seat on the village council for the better part of a decade; the position offers no direct benefit but costs him a morning at dawn every few days, which he has learned to accept as the price of having a voice in matters that affect the fields.
