# Otto

Age: 62. Village elder in Brunnfeld, Elder's House.

Otto has governed Brunnfeld for twenty-two years through a combination of genuine competence and a talent for making his self-interest align with the village's often enough that no one has successfully organized against him. He collects taxes on behalf of the distant lord, keeps a percentage for village administration, and spends what remains with enough visibility to remind people he is necessary. He knows exactly who is behind on rent and uses that knowledge with care, not cruelty — a man who feels cornered becomes unpredictable, and Otto prefers predictability. He is beginning to feel his age in the length of the accounting days and has started watching Ulrich and Heinrich with an appraiser's eye, not yet sure which of them might be useful. He chairs the village council, which by tradition includes the miller, blacksmith, baker, and a representative farmer — the four trades the village cannot function without.
