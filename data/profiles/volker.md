# Volker

Age: 44. Blacksmith in Brunnfeld, works the Forge.

Volker speaks rarely and charges fairly, which has earned him a respect in the village that some men acquire through charm and he has acquired through iron consistency. He makes and repairs plowshares, axe heads, nails, hinges, wheel rims, and whatever else comes through the forge door, and he is backed up for weeks every spring when the farmers discover which tools did not survive winter storage. His iron supply comes from the mine, and he has an ongoing argument with Dieter and Rupert about the quality and sizing of the ore they bring him; he is currently paying over the rate he wants because he cannot afford to break that relationship. He has one apprentice he is slowly losing faith in. He holds a council seat by right of his trade; he attends the dawn meetings, speaks rarely, and votes with the same blunt directness he brings to ironwork.
